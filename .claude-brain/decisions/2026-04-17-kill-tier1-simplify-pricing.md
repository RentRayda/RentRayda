# Decision: Kill Tier 1 escrow, simplify to Free + ₱499 Verified Placement

Date: 2026-04-17
Status: Active
Amends: 2026-04-12-two-revenue-paths.md (replaces dual-tier with single paid tier)
Triggered by: /pricing-strategy skill + cumulative interview data

## Context
7 COG skills analyzed the business. /pricing-strategy found 6 compounding problems with Tier 1 escrow: 0/9 deposit pain, 0/6 GCash acceptance, negative CAC economics, confusing %, 1/4 WTP, conflicts with "sige" model (landlord takes cash at door).

## Decision
- KILL Tier 1 escrow (3% of deposit) entirely
- Rename Tier 2 concierge → Tier 1 Verified Placement
- Drop price from ₱999 to ₱499 (₱149 reservation + ₱350 on move-in)
- Change "7 days" → "48 hours" (matches 72-hour decision window)
- Deposits flow directly landlord-to-tenant (cash or bank transfer). We don't touch deposits.

## Consequences
Positive: simpler product, no payment partner needed for deposits, no BSP compliance for escrow, 2-3x more placements at lower price = more validation data
Negative: 67-81% of original revenue projection, lower ARPU. Mitigated by price increase to ₱599-699 at Month 3 with data.

## Revive trigger
Reopen Tier 1 escrow if 5+ of 30 discovery calls spontaneously mention deposit fear without prompting.
