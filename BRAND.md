# RentRayda Brand Book
Version 1.0 / April 2026

---

## 1. Who We Are

RentRayda is a rental connection app built for the Philippine informal housing market.

We exist because thousands of provincial workers relocate to Metro Manila every year for BPO jobs, and the first thing they face is a broken rental system. Facebook groups full of fake listings. Agents who collect deposits and vanish. Landlords who refuse strangers because they have no way to verify who they are talking to.

RentRayda sits between landlords and tenants. We verify both sides before they ever exchange a phone number. That is the entire product. Not property management. Not payments. Not a social feed. Just trust, then connection.

Our users are real people making real decisions about where to sleep. We treat that seriously.

## 2. The Tarsier

Our mascot is a Philippine tarsier. A small, wide-eyed primate found only in the Visayas and Mindanao. It is endemic to the Philippines, which matters because so is this problem.

The tarsier has the largest eyes relative to body size of any mammal. That is the metaphor. RentRayda watches so our users do not have to. Big eyes, small body. Sees everything, threatens nothing.

The tarsier illustration style is rounded, friendly, and approachable. White silhouette on blue. It should feel like a character you trust, not a corporate logo. The tarsier sits upright, facing forward, eyes wide open. It is alert but calm.

Use the tarsier as:
- App icon (head only, no wordmark)
- Full lockup with "rent rayda" wordmark beneath
- Background element in hero sections (3D animated version on web)
- Watermark or subtle branding in empty states

Never distort, rotate, or recolor the tarsier outside the approved palette.

## 3. Logo

### The Wordmark

"rent rayda" is always lowercase. Two words. Set in TANNimbus, our brand display font. The name is a play on "rent radar" spoken with a Filipino accent. Keep it lowercase everywhere. It is informal by design.

### Logo Variants

| Variant | Use Case |
|---|---|
| Full lockup (tarsier + wordmark) | Splash screen, web hero, app store listing |
| Icon only (tarsier head) | App icon, favicon, small avatars |
| Wordmark only | Navigation bars, footers, compact spaces |
| Mono white | On dark or photographic backgrounds |
| Mono dark | On light backgrounds when blue is unavailable |

### Clear Space

Maintain a minimum clear space equal to the height of the tarsier's ear around all sides of the logo. No text, icons, or visual elements should intrude on this space.

### Minimum Size

- Full lockup: 120px wide minimum
- Icon only: 32px minimum
- Wordmark only: 80px wide minimum

## 4. Typography

RentRayda uses two typefaces. That is it. No fallback display fonts, no seasonal swaps, no "just this once" exceptions.

### TAN Nimbus (Display / Brand)

A serif display font with personality. Warm, slightly vintage, and distinctive. Used for anything that should feel like the brand speaking directly.

**Use for:**
- The "rent rayda" wordmark
- Hero headlines and section titles
- Landing page statements
- Celebration and ceremony screens

**Never use for:**
- Body text, descriptions, or long-form content
- Button labels
- Form inputs or error messages
- Anything below 18px

### Noto Sans Osage (Body / UI)

A clean, geometric sans-serif. Excellent legibility on budget Android screens at small sizes. Neutral enough to carry UI text without competing with TAN Nimbus.

**Use for:**
- All body text, descriptions, and paragraphs
- Button labels and CTAs
- Form inputs, labels, and helper text
- Navigation items and metadata
- Timestamps, captions, and badges

### Type Scale

| Level | Size | Font | Weight | Use |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Display | 32px | TAN Nimbus | 400 | Hero headlines, celebrations |
| Headline | 24px | TAN Nimbus | 400 | Screen titles, section headers |
| Title | 20px | TAN Nimbus | 400 | Card headers, page names |
| Subhead | 18px | Noto Sans Osage | 600 | Prices, emphasis |
| Body | 16px | Noto Sans Osage | 400 | Default text |
| Label | 14px | Noto Sans Osage | 500 | Buttons, form labels |
| Caption | 12px | Noto Sans Osage | 400 | Timestamps, helper text |

**Hard rules:**
- Never render text below 12px. Budget Android phones at 720p make anything smaller unreadable.
- Body text is always 16px minimum (WHO mobile readability standard).
- Maximum line length: 45 characters on mobile, 75 on web.

### The Verified Green Rule

The verified badge is the single most important trust signal in the product. It is always green (`#16A34A`). Never brand blue. Never any other color. The reason: green is a universal "safe / approved" signal that works across cultures. Brand blue could be confused with a generic UI element. When a user sees green on RentRayda, it means one thing and one thing only: this person has been checked.

## 6. Voice and Tone

### Principles

**Direct.** Say what you mean in as few words as possible. Our users are scanning, not reading. Many are on their commute or between shifts.

**Warm.** We are not a government form. We are the friend who already lives in the city and knows how things work. Approachable but not childish.

**Protective.** Every piece of copy should reinforce that we are watching out for the user. Not in a surveillance way. In a "we got your back" way.

**Honest.** If something failed, say it failed. If verification takes 48 hours, say 48 hours. No corporate softening. No "we appreciate your patience."

### Examples

| Context | Do | Do Not |
|---|---|---|
| Verification pending | "We are reviewing your documents. This usually takes 1 to 2 business days." | "Thank you for your patience! Our team is working diligently on your verification." |
| Connection accepted | "You are now connected. Here is their phone number." | "Congratulations! A new connection has been established between you and your match." |
| Error state | "Something went wrong. Try again." | "Oops! An unexpected error occurred. We apologize for the inconvenience." |
| Empty listings | "No listings in this area yet. Check back soon." | "It looks like there are no results matching your search criteria at this time." |
| CTA button | "Get verified" | "Start your verification journey" |

### Language Rules

- Write in English. No Taglish mixing in the product UI.
- Use "you" and "your" freely. Talk to the user, not about them.
- Avoid jargon. Say "phone number" not "contact information." Say "government ID" not "valid identification document."
- Keep button text to 3 words or fewer when possible.
- Error messages: what went wrong + what to do about it. Two sentences maximum.

## 7. Imagery

### Photography Style

RentRayda deals with real spaces. Bedspaces, shared rooms, small apartments. The photography should be honest.

- Natural lighting. No studio setups or HDR processing.
- Show the actual space. If the room is small, show that it is small.
- Include context: the hallway, the shared bathroom door, the view from the window.
- People in photos should look like they live there, not like they are posing for a lifestyle magazine.

We are not selling aspiration. We are selling honesty. A tenant should look at a listing photo and think "that is exactly what I will walk into."

### Illustration Style

When illustrations are needed (empty states, onboarding, celebrations), they should match the tarsier's visual language:

- Rounded shapes, soft edges
- Flat with minimal shading
- Brand blue as the primary illustration color
- Friendly and calm, never busy or cluttered
- Consistent stroke weight (2px at 24px icon size)

## 8. The Verified Badge

This deserves its own section because it is the heart of the product.

### Anatomy

The verified badge is a pill-shaped element containing:
1. A shield-check icon (from the RaydaIcon set)
2. The word "Verified" in 11px micro text

### Specifications

- Background: `#DCFCE7` (light green)
- Border: `1px solid #86EFAC`
- Text color: `#16A34A`
- Icon color: `#16A34A`
- Height: 28px
- Border radius: full (pill)
- Font: Noto Sans Osage, 11px, semibold

### Rules

- The badge appears on every verified landlord card, every verified tenant card, and every listing from a verified landlord.
- It is always green. Never brand blue. Never gray. Never any other color.
- It always contains both the icon and the text. Icon-only badges are not allowed because they are ambiguous at small sizes.
- It is not interactive. You cannot tap or click a verified badge. It is a status indicator, not a button.
- Unverified users do not get a "not verified" badge. The absence of the badge is the signal.

## 9. What We Are Not

These are brand boundaries. If a feature, design, or piece of copy crosses these lines, stop and reconsider.

- **We are not a payment platform.** No GCash buttons. No "pay rent here." No escrow. We connect people. They handle money themselves.
- **We are not a broker marketplace.** No agent profiles. No commission structures. No "featured listings" for pay.
- **We are not a social network.** No feeds, no likes, no followers, no comments. The only interaction is the connection request.
- **We are not Airbnb.** No star ratings. No reviews. No superhost badges. Verification is binary: you are verified or you are not.
- **We are not selling luxury.** Our design should feel clean and trustworthy, not premium or aspirational. Our users earn between 15,000 and 30,000 pesos a month. Respect that.
