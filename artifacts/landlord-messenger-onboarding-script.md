# Landlord Messenger Onboarding Script

**Purpose:** Step-by-step script for onboarding a new landlord via Facebook Messenger. The landlord never downloads the app during this flow — we do the listing creation on their behalf using photos and details they send us.

**Use case:** Miguel walks a barangay in Ugong/Kapitolyo, spots a "for rent" tarp, messages the Facebook profile or calls the number on the tarp, then switches the conversation to Messenger.

**Tone:** Warm, respectful, mabait. Use "po/opo." Taglish natural. NEVER formal corporate.

**Time target:** 15-20 minutes total, split across the landlord's schedule. Never rush.

---

## STAGE 1: The opening (send immediately after connection)

```
Hi po, si Miguel po ito from RentRayda 😊

Nakita po namin yung tarp ninyo sa [Ugong / Kapitolyo / San Antonio / Oranbo] tungkol sa [bedspace/room/apartment] for rent.

RentRayda po is a free service na tumutulong mag-list ng rental at maghanap ng verified na tenant — walang bayad sa landlord po, sa tenant lang. BPO workers po ang main market namin, yung mga fresh hire sa Concentrix, Accenture, Teleperformance.

Pwede po kayong tingnan sa rentrayda.com kung gusto niyo mag-check muna.

Interesado po ba kayong i-try? Libre po talaga, at hindi po kami connected sa BIR o government 🙏
```

**Why these exact words:**
- "Libre po talaga" addresses universal landlord pain (0/5 interviews wanted paid platform)
- "Hindi po kami connected sa BIR" directly addresses the #1 killer fear we identified in psychographic research
- "BPO workers po ang main market" signals tenant quality (regular income, corporate accountability)
- Emoji softens the outreach — matches target demographic communication norms
- Link to website gives them autonomy to verify, doesn't demand action

## STAGE 2: If they respond "Ano 'yan?" / "Paano yan?"

```
Simple lang po 🙏

1. Pagkuha po kayo ng picture ng unit ninyo (4-6 photos lang, maging yung kusina, CR, at kwarto)
2. I-send niyo lang po dito sa Messenger
3. Ako na po mag-list para sa inyo sa RentRayda app — you don't need to download anything

Pag may interesado na tenant, ipapakita ko po sa inyo ang profile niya (verified ID, nasaan siya nagtatrabaho, ano budget). Kayo po ang magde-decide kung papayag or hindi.

Pag pumayag kayo, kayo na po mag-usap via phone or Messenger. Kami hindi na po apektado. Yung deposit at rent, direkta po sa inyo — bank transfer o cash, kayo po bahala. Hindi po kami humahawak ng pera ninyo.

Interested po ba kayong subukan?
```

**Why these exact words:**
- "You don't need to download anything" — removes the single biggest landlord friction we identified
- "Verified ID, nasaan siya nagtatrabaho, ano budget" — offers her the tenant screening she wants (L4, L5 requested this)
- "Kayo po ang magde-decide" — respects her authority, doesn't strip control
- "Direkta po sa inyo — bank transfer o cash" — addresses BSP concerns AND reinforces trust
- "Kami hindi na po apektado" — signals we're not getting in between her and tenant relationship

## STAGE 3: Guarantee-lite pitch (after rapport, before listing creation)

Once the landlord has expressed interest (said yes or asked "paano yan?" and you've explained), introduce the guarantee-lite. This is the trust anchor that differentiates RentRayda from Facebook groups.

**If the landlord was referred by a barangay captain**, open with the referral before the pitch:

```
Si Kapitan [name] po ang nag-refer sa amin sa inyo. Maraming salamat po sa kanya — gusto niya po matulungan yung mga landlord sa barangay na makahanap ng magandang tenant.
```

Then deliver the guarantee-lite pitch:

```
Ate/Kuya, ang tenants na isesend namin sa inyo, verified na po — may PhilSys ID, confirmed na employed, at may record ng on-time payment. Kung umalis sila within 3 months, hahanap kami ng kapalit within 7 days, libre po. Mas maganda po sila kaysa random sa Facebook.
```

**If she asks "Talaga ba yan?":**

```
Opo. Kasi po, every tenant na ipapadala namin sa inyo — ine-check namin ang ID, employment, at history. Hindi po basta-basta. Kaya confident kami mag-offer ng replacement guarantee — kasi alam namin na maganda yung tenant from the start.

Pero syempre po, 'yung guarantee is about finding a replacement — hindi po kami nag-cocover ng rent o deposit. Kami ang maghahanap ng bagong tenant within 7 days, libre po.
```

**Why these exact words:**
- "Verified na po — may PhilSys ID, confirmed na employed" gives concrete proof, not vague promises
- "Kung umalis sila within 3 months, hahanap kami ng kapalit within 7 days" is the hook — no other platform offers this
- "Libre po" reinforces the zero-cost positioning
- "Mas maganda po sila kaysa random sa Facebook" directly compares to the channel she currently uses
- The barangay captain mention (when applicable) leverages existing community trust — the captain's endorsement carries more weight than any marketing

**Tracking (for founder):**
After delivering the pitch, record the landlord's reaction in the pipeline tracker:
- **Excited** — "Ah talaga? Ang ganda naman!" / asks follow-up questions / immediately more engaged
- **Neutral** — acknowledges but doesn't react strongly / "Sige, okay"
- **Skeptical** — "Paano kung hindi kayo makahanap?" / questions the guarantee / seems doubtful
- **Rejected** — "Hindi ko kailangan yan" / dismisses the offer / not interested

This data feeds into Prompt 42 (formal guarantee-lite launch). Target: >60% excited/positive before formalizing.

## STAGE 4: If yes — collecting listing details

Send this as a single message so she can respond in one batch:

```
Salamat po 🙏 Mag-collect lang po ako ng details para magandang maging listing ninyo.

**Tungkol sa Unit:**
1. Bedspace po ba, private room, or buong apartment?
2. Ilang tao po maximum pwede tumira?
3. Magkano po ang renta kada buwan?
4. Magkano po ang deposit (usually 1-2 months)?
5. Meron po bang advance? Magkano?

**Tungkol sa Kondisyon:**
6. Nasaan po exactly? (barangay at kalye na lang po, walang exact address)
7. Pang-ilang floor po? May elevator po ba?
8. Kasama po ba yung tubig at kuryente sa renta, or bukod?
9. Pwede po ba pet? May curfew po ba?

**Mga Amenities (check kung meron):**
10. Aircon / Electric fan / Maintanance lang
11. WiFi po ba meron?
12. Washing machine / laundry area?
13. Parking po ba meron?
14. Malapit po ba sa MRT-3 Ortigas, Concentrix, Accenture, Teleperformance?

**Picture:**
15. Pwede po ba 4-6 photos? Kusina, CR, kwarto, at exterior ng building (para makita ng tenant)

Sagot niyo na lang po isa-isa, hindi po need sabay. Relax lang po 😊
```

**Why each field:**
- Fields 1-5: pricing is what tenants filter on first
- Fields 6-7: location precision matters for BPO office proximity but we don't give exact address publicly (safety)
- Field 8: "kasama ba tubig at kuryente" is the #1 hidden-cost question every BPO renter asks
- Field 9: pet/curfew policies filter mismatched tenants early
- Fields 10-14: amenities that actually matter to BPO workers (night shift = needs aircon + WiFi)
- Field 15: photos are what sell the listing — no photos, no bookings

## STAGE 5: After she sends photos — quality check and polish

**If photos are blurry, dim, or only show one angle:**

```
Maganda na po yung unit ❤️ Pwede po ba ulitin mo yung [specific photo]? Medyo malabo po kasi at baka hindi maganda makita ng tenant. Kapag tanghali po, mas maganda yung ilaw pag kunan — natural light.

Isa pa pong suggestion: kapag may 1-2 pictures ng labas ng building (yung gate or facade), mas na-co-comfort yung tenant — alam niya sanung hitsura pag dumating siya.
```

**If photos are great:**

```
Perpekto po! 😊 Magaganda yung photos. I-prep ko na po listing ninyo ngayon.

Bibigyan po kita ng preview bago i-publish para ma-check niyo muna. 15-20 mins po.
```

## STAGE 6: Preview before publish

Create the listing in the admin dashboard using the details she sent. Take screenshots of:
- The listing card as it will appear to tenants
- The listing detail view

Send to her with this message:

```
Heto po yung preview ng listing ninyo 🏠

[screenshot of listing card]
[screenshot of listing detail]

Tama po ba lahat ng details? May gusto po kayong palitan? Halimbawa:
- Price mas mababa or mas mataas
- Amenities na nakalimutan
- Photo na pahalinhan

Sagot po kayo, ako na po mag-edit para sa inyo 🙏
```

**Why this step matters:**
- Landlord has full control, doesn't feel dictated to
- She sees exactly what tenants will see — no surprises
- Gives her psychological ownership over the listing
- Small corrections are easy now, painful post-launch

## STAGE 7: Verification request (after she approves the preview)

```
Salamat po! Bago i-publish, required po ng RentRayda ang verification para maging "Verified Landlord" badge kayo — mas madali ma-attract ng magandang tenant pag may badge 🙏

Kailangan po:
1. Valid ID ninyo (PhilID, driver's license, passport — any one)
2. Proof na kayo ang may-ari ng unit (deed of sale, title, or utility bill na nasa pangalan ninyo)

Pwede po photo ng phone lang, hindi kailangan scanner. I-send niyo lang po dito sa Messenger — encrypted po yan at kami lang ang makakakita.

Tanong po ba kayo kung bakit required?
- Para ma-protect kayo mula sa scammers na nag-po-pose na landlord
- Para trusted kayo ng BPO tenants na sobrang conscious sa safety
- Kami po nagko-compile at hindi isi-share sa BIR o iba pang agency — RA 10173 Data Privacy Act po ang sinusunod namin
```

**Why this works:**
- Anchors the verification in HER benefit (protection, trust) not ours
- Phone photo is explicit permission to skip scanner
- "Kami po nagko-compile at hindi isi-share" addresses the #1 landlord fear we found
- RA 10173 reference signals we know what we're doing legally

## STAGE 8: Post-verification confirmation

Once you've approved her verification in admin:

```
Verified na po kayo! ✅ Buhay na rin po yung listing:

rentrayda.com/listings/[id]

Mga susunod na mangyayari po:
1. Pag may interesado, you'll receive a Messenger message from me saying: "May interesado po si [Name] sa listing ninyo. Verified po siya, BPO worker sa [Company], budget niya [₱X]. Pwede niyo ba siyang pag-usapan?"
2. Pag pumayag po kayo, ibibigay namin number niya sa inyo at kayo na mag-coordinate.
3. Pag may nag-commit, diretso po ang deposit sa inyo — bank transfer o cash, kayo po bahala. Hindi kami humahawak ng pera.
4. 100% libre po para sa inyo, from start to finish.

May tanong po ba? Nandito lang po ako 🙏
```

## STAGE 9: First inquiry routing (within 24-48h of listing going live)

When a verified tenant submits an inquiry:

```
Hi po Mrs. [Name]! 😊

May interested sa unit ninyo:

**Tenant Profile:**
- Name: [First name only]
- Age: [X]
- Work: [Company, position]
- Budget: ₱[X] (match po sa listing)
- Move-in: [date]
- Verified ID: ✅ Yes
- Verified Employment: ✅ Yes

Message niya: "[inquiry text]"

Pwede po ba siyang pag-usapan? Pag sabihin niyo po YES, ibibigay ko number niya at pwede na kayong mag-coordinate directly.
```

## STAGE 10: Post-placement follow-up (48h after tenant moves in)

```
Kumusta po, Mrs. [Name]? ❤️

Narinig ko po na successful yung move-in ni [Tenant first name]. Maraming salamat po sa trust!

Tanong lang po:
1. May problema po ba sa tenant o sa deposit?
2. May bago po bang vacancy ninyo na pwede namin i-list? Yung malapit na may matatapos na kontrata?
3. Kung may kakilala po kayong ibang landlord na may vacancy, pwede po namin sila i-contact? Free din po silang makaka-list 🙏

Kung walang concerns po, enjoy niyo lang po yung successful placement 😊
```

**Why this step:**
- Confirms no disputes (early warning for any issues)
- Opens door for repeat listings (sticky relationship)
- Referral ask timed at moment of maximum satisfaction
- Offers her agency to say no — not pushy

## Escalation scripts for edge cases

### If she says "Wala akong Messenger"
```
Walang problema po. Pwede rin po via SMS/Viber/phone. Ano po mas comfortable sa inyo?
```

### If she says "Ayaw ko magpa-kuha ng ID"
```
Naiintindihan ko po. Ang issue po kasi, madaming scammer ngayon na pretend landlord — kaya yung BPO tenants ay nag-hahanap ng verified badge. Pag walang verification, less likely po ma-match.

Kung wala pa po kayo ready i-share, pwede muna i-list without verification, pero mas slow po ang inquiries. Kayo po bahala.
```

### If she says "Ano mangyayari sa BIR?"
```
Important po yan. Let me be direct:

1. RentRayda hindi po nag-re-report ng anything sa BIR. Hindi kami tax collector, hindi kami government agent.
2. Ang records namin ay para sa amin lang (verification, customer support).
3. Sa legal sense, kahit cash or digital, rental income ay technically taxable — pero yan pagitan ninyo at ng BIR, hindi namin pake.
4. Yung deposit at rent payment ay transaction between you and tenant — bank transfer o cash, kayo po ang bahala. Hindi po yan sa amin.

Ikaw po mismo ang decide kung magpa-register sa BIR, hindi kami magtutulak. 🙏
```

### If she says "Magkano talaga bayad ko?"
```
₱0 po talaga. Forever. No hidden fees, no percentage, no subscription. 

Kami kumukuha ng ₱499 flat fee SA TENANT side (hindi sa inyo) para sa Verified Placement service. Hindi sa inyo — yung tenant po ang magbabayad. Kayo po buo yung ₱[deposit amount] na nakareceive.
```

### If she says "Binebenta niyo ba yung info ko?"
```
Hindi po talaga. Ang data ninyo ay protected under RA 10173 (Data Privacy Act). Hindi namin binebenta, hindi namin shino-share sa anyone without your permission.

Yung visible lang sa tenant ay: inyong first name, verified badge, at general area ng unit (barangay level, walang exact address). Iba — hidden po.

Pag gusto niyo tanggalin ang data ninyo, sabihin niyo lang at 30 days ako mag-delete.
```

## Operational notes for whoever runs this script

1. **Response time target: within 1 hour of her message during her waking hours (6am-10pm).** Landlords 45-70 check Messenger in bursts. If you reply fast during her active window, conversation flows. If you reply 12 hours later, you're restarting cold.

2. **Never use bullet points or hashtags in messages.** Native Messenger UX is conversational. Bullet points feel formal and corporate.

3. **Always address her as "Mrs. [Name]" or "Ate [Name]" or "Nanay [Name]" depending on age signal.** Default to "Mrs." when uncertain.

4. **If she takes >48h to respond, send one gentle follow-up — then stop.** "Hi po, Mrs. [Name] — kumusta po? Nandito lang po kapag na-a-available na kayo 🙏" Then silence. Don't double-tap.

5. **Document every landlord conversation in the admin dashboard.** Name, Messenger link, unit count, current status, next action, last contact. This becomes the landlord CRM.

6. **Track onboarding funnel:** Reached out → Responded → Agreed to try → Sent photos → Verified → Listing live → First inquiry → First placement. You need to know your drop-off rate at each step to improve.

7. **After 5-10 successful onboardings, train a second person on this script.** At 50+ active landlords, the founder cannot be the only one doing this — handoff becomes a bottleneck.

---

## When to graduate to managed agent assistance

At ~50 active landlords (estimated Month 3 if validation passes):
- Use managed agents for follow-up reminder sequences (NOT initial onboarding — that stays human)
- Agent handles: "Mrs. Cora, kumusta po yung tenant niyo si Maria?" at Day 14, Day 30, Day 60
- Agent handles: "May bago po kayong vacancy?" when a listing gets auto-paused after 30 days inactive
- Founder handles: any emotional conversation, dispute, complaint, referral thank-you

Context handoff: agent has read access to the landlord's full conversation history + listing history so context doesn't reset.
