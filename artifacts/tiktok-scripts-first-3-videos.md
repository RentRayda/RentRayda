# TikTok Scripts — First 3 Videos

**Purpose:** Launch TikTok as primary awareness channel with content calibrated to the psychographic profile of female BPO new hires, 20-26, provincial migrants.

**⚠️ SCOPE NOTE:** These scripts lead with the female BPO narrator because that demographic has our sharpest empathy signal. The PLATFORM serves all genders, all employer types, all provincial migrants. Once first 3 videos prove the channel works, add variants:
- Video 4+: male BPO new hire perspective (same scam story, different voice)
- Video 5+: student perspective (DLSU/UP/PUP fresh enrollee)
- Video 6+: fresh grad in healthcare/banking perspective
Do NOT restrict future content creation to female BPO only. That's where we start, not where we stop.

**Core insight from research:** Nano-influencer content in "dorm room contexts" has strongest trust signal. TikTok commands 40 hrs/month for this demographic. Lead with safety + scam-avoidance — these are the emotional hooks.

**Format rules:**
- 60-90 seconds max (TikTok algorithm favors completion)
- Face-to-camera in natural setting (bedspace, BPO cafeteria, MRT)
- Taglish throughout — never pure English (feels corporate), never pure Tagalog (feels provincial)
- Captions on-screen (80% watch with sound off)
- Hook in first 3 seconds (scroll-stopper)
- CTA in last 5 seconds
- No music-driven dances. This is informational/emotional content.

**Creator profile:** Ideally a female BPO worker aged 22-26 who can be genuine on camera. Pay nano-influencer rate (₱2-5K per video, or product/placement exchange). NOT a paid actor.

## VIDEO 1: "Almost na-scam ako ng ₱10,000" (Near-scam story)

**Hook (0-3s):** Close-up, slightly emotional, direct-to-camera

> "Grabe, grabe, grabe — halos na-scam ako ng ten-thousand pesos last week."

**Build (3-15s):** Narrative, hands gesturing

> "So, nag-reply ako sa isang bedspace ad sa Facebook, yung malapit sa Ortigas, mura, ₱5,500 only, kasama daw tubig at kuryente."
> 
> "Nag-DM yung landlord na 'reserve mo na, may five other interested, send mo muna ₱10K sa GCash for deposit para hindi ma-give ko sa iba.'"

**Turn (15-30s):** Lean in, quieter

> "Luckily, something felt off — yung hitsura ng unit sa picture, parang bahay sa Airbnb, hindi bedspace. So nag-Google ko yung phone number."

**Reveal (30-45s):** Show phone screen — search result

> "Same number. Different person. Different listing. Three barangays away. Lahat may nag-pa-reserve na."
> 
> "Scammer siya. At least four other girls na na-scam din."

**Resolution (45-60s):** Face to camera, calmer

> "Hindi ako nagpadala. Pero grabe yung takot ko. Ten-thousand pesos — yan yung remittance ko sa family for the whole month."
> 
> "Dalawang bagay lang tuturo ko sa inyo ngayon:"
> 
> "Una, NEVER magpadala ng pera before niyo nakita yung unit in person."
> 
> "Pangalawa, meron app called RentRayda na nag-ve-verify ng landlords. Verified badge — nakita niyo? Yun lang inuuna ko ngayon pag naghahanap."

**CTA (60-65s):** On-screen text + voiceover

> "Hindi sponsored 'to — just wanted to share. Check niyo — link sa bio."

**On-screen captions throughout:**
- (0-3s) "HALOS NA-SCAM AKO NG ₱10K 😭"
- (15-30s) "something felt off..."
- (30-45s) "SAME NUMBER. IBA IBANG LISTINGS."
- (60-65s) "Check niyo RentRayda sa bio 🔗"

**Hashtags:**
#BPOlife #bedspaceManila #ScamAlertPH #Pasiglife #ConcentrixBPO #BPOnewbie #rentalscam #FilipinaBPO

**Why this script works:**
- Leads with the #1 fear (scam = losing remittance money) from primary psychographic research
- Storytelling format matches TikTok native content
- "Hindi sponsored" creates authenticity (even if it IS a brand-sponsored creator)
- The resolution offers a concrete tool (RentRayda) without feeling like an ad
- Specific numbers (₱10K, 4 other victims) create credibility

## VIDEO 2: "First night sa Manila: where do I even sleep??" (Urgency + solution)

**Hook (0-3s):** Shot from bus window or MRT, voiceover

> "Unang beses ko sa Manila. Training starts in 4 days. Wala akong kakilala. Saan ako matutulog ngayon?"

**Situation (3-20s):** Cut to her sitting in a coffee shop with phone, hands showing Facebook scrolling

> "Okay, plan was simple: find bedspace near Concentrix Ortigas, mga ₱6K budget, female-only. Yan lang. Dapat simple lang 'di ba?"
> 
> "Pero — nag-message ako sa 15 na ads sa Facebook. Ilan ang nag-reply? Three. Tatlo. Yung iba hindi na pala available, yung iba sabi nila 'reserve first before viewing.'"

**Pain (20-35s):** Slightly frustrated, looking at phone

> "Ilang oras na ako dito. Battery ko 23%. Phone na walang WiFi. Uwi ako sa province? Sayang yung pamasahe. Stay here? Saan?"

**Turn (35-50s):** Calmer, back to camera

> "Then someone from my training batch — yung groupchat sa Messenger — nag-share ng something. RentRayda. Sabi niya verified yung mga listings, at they can help you find a place in 48 hours."

**Resolution (50-70s):** Show phone screen — RentRayda app / listing page

> "Browse ako — 30-plus listings near Ortigas, lahat verified, may photos at may badge. Filter ko: female-only, under ₱7K, near MRT — may nine options."
> 
> "Nag-message ako — someone replied in like 20 minutes. By end of day, may scheduled viewing na ako for Saturday."

**CTA (70-80s):** Face to camera

> "Not paid to say this — pero if you're a provincial new hire and you're freaking out about housing — ito talaga yung shortcut. Check bio. Tapos dinagdag niyo yung batchmate niyo dito, para hindi sila ma-scam din."

**On-screen captions:**
- (0-3s) "FIRST NIGHT SA MANILA 😰"
- (3-20s) "15 messages. 3 replies."
- (35-50s) "then my batchmate sent me this..."
- (70-80s) "Link sa bio 🔗 Share sa batchmate niyo 🙏"

**Hashtags:**
#BPOnewhire #provincetoManila #freshgrad #BPOlife #Concentrix #Accenture #Teleperformance #Ortigaslife #bedspacephilippines #Manilahousing

**Why this script works:**
- Captures the 72-hour decision window we identified in psychographic research
- Mirrors the exact emotional arc of a provincial migrant's first day (from research synthesis)
- "Batchmate on Messenger shared this" matches the kakilala/peer referral trust pattern (strongest trust signal for this demographic)
- The "share with batchmate" CTA triggers Messenger virality (the transaction platform)
- Budget (₱6K) and location (near Concentrix Ortigas) are specific enough to feel real

## Posting cadence and rollout plan

**Week 1:**
- Day 1 (Monday 7pm): Video 1 (scam story — highest emotional pull, recruits followers)
- Day 3 (Wednesday 7pm): Video 2 (urgency + solution — drives traffic to RentRayda)
- Day 5 (Friday 7pm): Video 3 (landlord humanization — deepens trust)

**Week 2-4:**
- 3 videos per week minimum (MWF 7pm optimal for BPO shift patterns)
- Alternate between: scam-avoidance stories, product demos, landlord interviews, tenant testimonials, BPO life content (tangential)
- Always include "link sa bio" — don't vary the CTA until data shows it's not working

**Engagement rules:**
- Reply to every comment within 6 hours during Week 1-2
- If someone comments "Legit ba 'to?" — this is the key trust-checking phrase from research. Respond with a specific verified listing URL
- Save high-intent commenters (asking about specific barangays, budgets) for direct Messenger outreach

**Success metrics:**
- Week 1 target: 10K combined views, 3 landing page conversions
- Week 2 target: 25K views, 8 conversions
- Week 4 target: 100K views, 25+ conversions
- If any single video gets <1K views after 48h, do NOT double-down on that format — test a new hook

**Content to NEVER make:**
- Videos selling RentRayda features (nobody cares about your features — they care about their problem)
- Videos with polished corporate voiceover (kills trust)
- Videos with English-only dialogue (feels alienating)
- Videos mocking landlords or tenants (respect is mandatory)
- Videos promising specific outcomes ("find a place in 48 hours guaranteed" — overpromise = refund risk)

## Warning: do NOT do these things

1. Do NOT post directly from the @rentrayda account with generic product marketing. Algorithm will bury it. Use creator-fronted content only.

2. Do NOT buy followers or engagement. TikTok algorithm is ruthless about inauthentic activity. One bought boost and you're shadowbanned.

3. Do NOT cross-post TikTok videos to Facebook with the watermark. Facebook deprioritizes TikTok-watermarked content heavily. Remove watermark before Facebook repost, or just make Facebook-native content separately.

4. Do NOT respond to hate comments or scam accusations. Ignore, block if severe. Engagement with negativity multiplies its reach.

5. Do NOT promise specific outcomes ("you'll find a place in 3 days!"). If we deliver in 5, we disappointed them. Under-promise, over-deliver.
